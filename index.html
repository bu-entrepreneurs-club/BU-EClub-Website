Club Membership

Club membership is a selective process that brings together the most passionate, innovative BU students.

Together, we hold small, bi-monthly meetings, bring in key figures and stakeholders, and hold each other accountable for our goals.

Our princicples operate on a two-way street paradigm, that is, everyone contributes to the club and everyone receives.

Terrier Startup Challenge

The Terrier Startup Challenge is an introductory program/competition to introduce students to entrepreneurship.

In the program, students are taske with forming a team, solving a real world problem using elements of entrepreneurship, and demoing their company at a demo day in hopes of winning first place.
